OVERVIEW

HNM was established in the year 1988 to cater to the pump, valve and other General Engineering Industries located abundantly across Coimbatore, a well recognized and respected Industrial destination in India.

Since 1988, HNM has developed personnel, facilities, materials, and processes required to recognize and meet its valuable customer’s rubber needs.

Our ISO 9001:2015 certified manufacturing facility, occupying 4000square feet of floor space, backed by 30 skilled and committed workforce dedicated to giving their best to the industry requirements.

With a unique combination of Knowledge, experience, State of –the-art equipments and quality manufacturing systems, HNM manufacture wide range of Rubber Products and Rubber to metal bonded products exactly as per the requirements of its clients.

WHAT WE DO

The products we manufacture, ranging from 0.5 Gms to 2000 Gms, are used for a wide variety of applications such as Pump & Valve, Automobile, Railways, Aerospace, Defense, Industrial Automation, White Goods, Mining, Process Industries, Material handling, General Engineering, Hardware, Building & Construction, Plumbing, etc.
